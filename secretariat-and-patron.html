  • Hong Kong Science & Technology Parks Corporation (HKSTPC)

  • HKSPTC provides facilities, services and a dynamic environment that enable companies to nurture ideas, innovate and develop; propelling Hong Kong towards a world-class hub for selected technologies - electronics, information technology and telecommunications, precision engineering, biotechnology and green technology.

    Besides, HKSTPC is the first incubator in Asia to be accredited as Soft Landings International Incubator by NBIA, with providing full-service incubation programmes (link is external) to assist start-ups during their vulnerable inception stages. Its mission to foster the growth of qualified technology and design based start-ups in our supported clusters, by providing a set of value-added services and financial support to nurture their development in areas of product/services, marketing and organization.
