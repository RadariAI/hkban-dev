  • Introduction

  • Established in 2010, the Hong Kong Venture Investment and Business Angel Network (HKBAN) is a leading association representing venture capital and angel investors in Hong Kong. Established to strengthen connectivity within the venture investment ecosystem, HKBAN serves as a vital platform for fostering innovation, knowledge sharing, and professional growth.

    Our mission is to bridge the gap between investors and the broader entrepreneurial ecosystem, creating a vibrant community that drives the advancement of the venture investment landscape. By facilitating meaningful connections and advocating for policy changes, we aim to enhance the significance of venture investment within Hong Kong's financial sector.

    At HKBAN, we are committed to elevating the professional skills of practitioners through curated events, workshops, and networking opportunities. We provide our members with the tools and insights needed to navigate the dynamic landscape of venture investing while reinforcing the transformative power of innovation and technology as essential drivers of economic progress. By supporting cutting-edge innovation and fostering a culture of technological advancement, HKBAN aims to position Hong Kong as a global hub for venture investment.

    As a catalyst for change, HKBAN is dedicated to building a thriving ecosystem that empowers all stakeholders within the venture investment community. Together, we are shaping the future of innovation and enhancing the importance of venture investment in Hong Kong and beyond.

    Join us in our journey to create a stronger, more connected, and innovative investment community.
