  • Privacy Policy Statement of Hong Kong Venture Investment and Business Angel Network Limited

    Updated: Feb 2025

    Privacy Policy Statement

    Hong Kong Venture Investment and Business Angel Network Limited (“HKBAN”) appreciates the privacy of our users and we are committed to fully implementing and complying with the data protection principles and all relevant provisions under Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486 of the laws of Hong Kong).

  • What we collect

  • When you visit our website at www.hkban.org (the “Site”), you need not provide us with any personally identifiable information about you (“Personal Information”). HKBAN is a sharing platform that connects entrepreneurs with our members and network of investors, advisors and professional service providers. If you wish for us to facilitate the connection, our primary area of service, we may need you to provide us with your Personal Information.

    This Private Policy Statement (the “Privacy Policy”) covers the use of the Site and services we provide. We recommend that you read this Privacy Policy carefully when using the Site and services. By using the Site, you agree to and accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you may leave the Site.

    You do not have to provide Personal Information to us for using the Site and we do not generally collect your Personal Information while you browse. When we collect Personal Information, we will provide you with our Personal Information Collection Statement, which is available by clicking here.

    HKBAN does not provide your Personal Information to any non-member third party without your prior written consent. HKBAN is dedicated to assisting young ventures in obtaining investment funding between seed and venture capital funding stages by holding regular Investment Matching Gatherings (“IMGs”) to help connect investors and young ventures in an investment-matching platform. If you wish to participate in such activities or otherwise use our services, it may be necessary for you to provide us with your Personal Information.

    Please note that the Privacy Policy is subject to update, and any such updates will be posted on this webpage.

  • Statement of Practice

  • 1. Information We Collect About You

    1.1 In general, you may visit the Site without telling us who you are or revealing any Personal Information about yourself. We do collect information relating to your navigation to the Site.

    1.2 When you visit this Site, a range of information is collected and processed for the purposes of navigation on this Site, or to make browsing more functional and easier. Such information includes but is not limited to:

    the operating system you use
    your IP address
    your browser type and language
    the type of computer or electronic device used in accessing the Site (such as desktop computer or portable device)
    your access times
    pages on www.hkban.org you may visit
    the content of any undeleted cookies that your browser previously accepted from us (see “Cookies” below)
    the referring website address, if any
    other relevant information

    1.3 “Cookies” are small files that a website transfers to your computer or electronic device to allow the website to remember specific information. When you visit the Site, we may assign your computer one or more cookies, to facilitate access to the Site and to personalise your online experience. Through the use of a cookie, we also may automatically collect information about your online activity on the Site, such as the web pages you visit, the links you click, and searches you conducted on the Site.

    1.4 Most browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies. While you will still be able to access most features of the Site, certain features may not work as well or may be unavailable to you if you do not accept cookies.

    1.5 We may request your Personal information if you intend to apply to become our member or otherwise use our services. Personal Information and other data we collect may include information such as your:

    given name and surname
    email address
    address or contact information
    employment or professional background
    business or investment activities and experience
    areas of investment interest if you are an investor and your experience in investment in such areas
    company, industry or investment needs if you are seeking investors
    other relevant information necessary or useful for us to link you with a counterparty

    The nature of Personal Information collected may vary depending on the features you use on this Site, such as registering as a member of HKBAN through the Site, subscribing for services, and opting in or out of newsletters or other communications.

    1.6 Please note that as a sharing platform, we will allow members and registered entrepreneurs who logged onto the Site limited access to other members’ email address. No Personal Information or other information of other members or registered entrepreneurs is accessible.

    2. How We Use the Information We Collect

    2.1 If HKBAN collects Personal Information, you agree that we may use information for any and all of the following purposes:

    inform you about and ask you to join IMGs or similar investment events
    invite you to matching platforms where HKBAN is the organizer or one of the organizers of such platforms
    inform you of potential companies whom you have shown an interest for investment so that they may contact you directly or you contacting them directly to follow up a connection
    inform other potential angel or VC investors who are interested to co-invest in the same potential investees that you have indicated an interest to co-invest
    inform you about angel, venture capital, investment, and entrepreneurial related events such as seminars, presentations, panel discussions/clinics, or conferences where we are the organizer, co-organizer, sponsor, supporting organization or partner of such event
    conduct research and analysis about your use of, or interest in, our services, or content, or products, services or content offered by others
    communicate with you by email, postal mail, and/or electronic devices about services that may be of interest to you
    enforce our terms and conditions
    manage our operation and perform functions as otherwise described to you at the time of collection

    2.2 In addition to Personal Information, we also collect, store, handle and analyse other information such as record of online activities through our Sites while you visit our website.

    2.3 We would like to use your Personal Information to send you information about our activities and services and we will obtain your consent to do so. If you do not want to receive such materials from us, please send us an email to enquiry@hkban.org. You can also unsubscribe from our mailing list via the link at the bottom of our emails.

    2.4 We will retain your Personal Information for as long as required to fulfil the purpose for which they were collected. Thereafter, we will delete the Personal Information in our storage.

    Please review “Your Choices About Collection and Use of Your Information” below. In addition, please refer to our Personal Information Collection Statement by clicking here.

    3. Your Choices about Collection and Use of Your Information

    3.1 You can choose not to provide us with your Personal Information, but that may result in your being unable to use certain features of our Site.

    3.2 You may choose at any time to cancel or terminate your authorisation to our use of your Personal Information and request that we delete your Personal Information in our storage. Please see “How to Contact Us” below or by clicking here.

    3.3 When we collect your Personal Information, we will obtain your consent in a clear, explicit and specific manner. You will be fully aware of what Personal Information we intend to use and the purposes of such usage (such as improving our services or enrolling you in IMGs); and the user of the Personal Information you provide (such as a potential investor, co-investor or investee company) and how such user may use your Personal Information.

    4. How You Can Access Your Information

    You have a right to access and correct any Personal Information about you that is incorrect or inaccurate and to update such Personal Information. To access, correct, or update your Personal Information, HKBAN members and entrepreneurs may do so directly on the Site by logging in and making such changes directly. For others, please email us at enquiry@hkban.org requesting access or by clicking here.

    5. How We Protect Your Personal Information

    5.1 We take security measures (including physical, electronic, and procedural measures) to help safeguard your Personal Information from unauthorised access and disclosure. Only authorised individuals are permitted to access Personal Information and they may do so only for permitted operational procedures.

    5.2 [In addition, we use encryption in the transmission of sensitive information between your system and ours and we use firewalls to help prevent unauthorised persons from gaining access to your Personal Information.]

    5.3 We want you to feel confident using our Site. However, no system can be completely secure. Therefore, although we take steps to secure your information, we do not promise, and you should not expect, that your Personal Information, searches, or other communications will always remain secure.

    5.4 You should also take care with how you handle and disclose your Personal Information and should avoid sending personal information through insecure email.

    5.5 [We have also appointed a Data Protection Officer to help us comply with applicable privacy and other laws and regulations and monitor our compliance with such laws. Our Data Protection Officer will also co-operate with relevant privacy supervisory agencies and represent us on issues regarding the Personal Information handling and related matters.]

    6. Outsourcing arrangements

    HKBAN does not have its own employed staff. In addition to providing HKBAN with office space, Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation (“HKSTP”) provides HKBAN with the administrative, operational and technical services we need to operate. HKSTP also developed and maintains the Site. HKSTP staff members handling HKBAN matters are bound to keep confidential any data they come into contact with against unauthorised access, use and retention.

    7. Links to Other Websites

    The Site may contain links to other websites that are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of other websites. This privacy statement applies only to the Site.

    8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    8.1 We will occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and services.

    8.2 When we post changes to this Privacy Policy, we will revise the “Updated” date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

    8.3 We recommend that you check the Site from time to time to keep abreast of changes in this Privacy Policy or any of our other policies.
